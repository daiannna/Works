Ninety One is a Kazakhstan boys band that debuted on September 1, 2015 under the label JUZ Entertainment, which includes: ALEM (Batyrkhan Malikov), ACE (Azamat Ashmakyn), ZAQ (Dulat Mukhov ametkaliyev) and BALA (Daniyar Kulumshin) [1]. The producer of the group is a member of the Kazakh musical group ORDA - Yerbolat Bedelkhan [2]. A.Z (Azamat Zenkaev) left the group on August 5, 2020.

On August 24, 2017, the film "Ninety One" was released, dedicated to the work and biography of the boyband; director of the film - Askar Uzabaev, producers - Asel Sadvakasova and Yerbolat Bedelkhan. The film "Ninety One" entered the top three of the Kazakhstan rental, during the premiere weekend it raised 32,672,000 tenge.

Ninety One producer Yerbolat Bedelhan directed a motivational series called "Қағаз кеме" ("Paper Boat"). The premiere took place on October 30 [10] on the NTK TV channel.
